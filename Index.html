Our Objective

Take the confusion out of selling a house! Avoid all the unecessary info and just see just how much is available for the bang for your buck. We want to empower you, the consumer.

Why We Chose this Problem

We chose to tackle the problem of lack of transparency in the housing market regarding online posts as it seemed really relevant in the times right now, and specifically in the region of the Greater Toronto Area as real estate is being snatched up as soon as possible. Properties are being sold within hours of being posted to MLS as the area is highly competitive as real estate is appreciating in value as high as 30% in just 6 months at locations such as Casa Loma, Yonge - St. Clair and the Annex. With these erratic price shifts, it is hard for new buyers to figure out whether they are getting a good deal on a property. New home buyers are also susceptible to the persuasion of realtors, and we believe that some listing agencies are not 100% transparent with the information that they have so that potential buyers are more likely to book a meeting with a realtor.

How We Built Our Solution

With a combination of web desgin knowledge, mathematical algorithims, and mapping apis, we used our skillset and knowledge, to create a web based map which displays houses and all relevant information in the Tornto area. However, unlike sites such as Zillow, or Realtor.ca, we don't leave our consumers in the dark by feeding them endless information. We take all the relevant information which adds or detracts to the value of a home and create a rating out of 100. Our Algorithim takes into account price per square foot, the number of bedrooms, bathrooms, parking, pool(s), and garage space to take the confusion out of home buying!
